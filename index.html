Current and Established Patients

Hello friends! Thank you for your trust and support since 2012. I appreciate you! I remain committed to serving your needs.

I have lifted the COVID vaccination requirements but encourage boosters and community-caring behavior. Many of my patients are at high risk, so I take disease transmission seriously.

My morning appointments have 15-minute intervals between appointment times for ventilation and cleaning (9-10am, 10:15-11:15am, and 11:30am-12:30pm). Afternoon slots (2-3pm and 3:30-4:30pm) have 30-minute intervals in between appointment slots for ventilation and cleaning. I continue wearing an N95 mask during treatments. You may choose whether to mask based on your comfort level.

Please do not come in if you are acutely ill, symptomatic, or have had recent contact with someone who is. If you have lingering symptoms such as a cough, even though otherwise feel recovered please wear a mask during treatment. There are no charges for last-minute cancellations due to illness.

For those who are sick or prefer not to come in, herbal consultations via video chat are available, with herbs shipped to your home. If you need help scheduling, email or text me with your name and desired appointment time.
